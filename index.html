The dynamic sidebar

How it works

Sidebars are often used in blogs, forums or wikis. One of the main design-trends is to have a sidebar that is consistent width height as the article on the side or main content. In order for it to stay consistent it needs to have the same height as the article content, and the article it self. An usual fix for that is to put the height to 100 percent viewport height or 100% on the height in css. And make it positioned fixed, to tag a long the article. However that doesn't fix the fact, if the sidebar-content are longer than the article or if the article content is heigher than the viewportheight.
